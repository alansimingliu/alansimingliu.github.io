About Me

My name is Alan and I am currently a senior studying Information Technology and Systems at The University of Texas at Dallas. I have developed a passion for data analytics and software development and look to be a dynamic and adaptable member to any team I am a part of. I look to eventually combine my interests in organizational psychology with my technical experience and move into project management.

Resume

Liberty Mutual Insurance

In the summer of 2018, I had the opportunity to be a software development intern for Liberty Mutual Insurance in Seattle, Washington. During that time there, I was able to work with a team of three other interns and under the guidance of three full time employees who guided us throughout the entire internship process. We developed in Javascript and React to improve the company's telematics data collecting tool for their car insurance holders. In addition to the project, I had the chance to participate in a hackathon sponsored by the company where my team and I build a social medial analysis tree to show how the company can improve their social media presence with the public.

Course Hero

During the fall semester of my sophomore year, I chance to be the first marketing intern at The University of Texas at Dallas. During my tenure, I was tasked to promote the brand to the students at the university by using social media and cultivating marketing events on campus. During my time as an intern, I was able to engage with over 600 students and increase their North Texas presence by 18 percent.

GOOGLE ADWORDS LOCAL
BUSINESS CAMPAIGN

For this project, my group and I were assigned to partner up with a local entrepreneur and perform SEO and SEM analysis for their company website. Our client was a social media influencer who catered his services towards international students looking to study abroad and obtain a degree in the United States. We promoted their website by using Google AdWords and other analytics tools for a period of ten weeks and saw a 3,500 dollar increase in overall business value. In addition, we were able to increase the click through rate by 7.7% and increased the female demographic from 15% to 28.55%.

IPREMIER CASE STUDY
CONSULTING

This project required my group and I to analyze a case study regarding the IT governance policies of a company names iPremier. This company was a victim of a distributed denial of service attack and did not follow proper protocol to mitigate the damage. In a twelve page report, my group and I recommend ways iPremier could improve its IT governance, update the IT infrastructure design, and what frameworks would yield the most effective results for their situation

American Airlines
Ticket Booking System

The American Airlines flight ticket booking system is a relatively arduous process that can present a number of difficulties to the users engaging with the interface. Our group was assigned to illustrate the business process model for AA and analyze any opportunities to make the process more efficient. Our recommendations would have decreased the average ticket purchase time by 14 percent, and to help showcase these changes, we programed a beta version of the new and improved system using Java.
